John Jacobsen

Researcher at the California Pacific Medical Center Research Institute and UC Berkeley Statistics graduate. I work at the intersection of biostatistics, causal inference, and health equity — with particular interest in aging, environmental epidemiology, and the politics of who gets counted in data.

Before statistics, I spent nearly a decade as a welder and shipfitter, and have been involved in labor and tenant organizing for most of my adult life. That background shapes how I think about research questions.
